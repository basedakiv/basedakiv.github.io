OUTREACH

As an American Space & Makerspace team, we are located in the heart of the STEAM community in Kazakhstan. Although Makerspace is located in Smart.point it is equivalent to Silicon Valley in Kazakhstan. Our outreach philosophy is rooted in the belief that we can directly impact our community through grassroots initiatives. To do this, we keep our lab open to visitors while we work and use it as a venue for outreach events. This not only keeps people engaged in the field of robotics, but also helps to develop an interest in FIRST programs.

Galaxy International school

Our recent presentation of the First Tech Challenge at Galaxy International School was a tremendous success, serving as a means to both motivate and engage students in the fields of FTC and STEM.Our interactive approach featured an entertaining Kahoot game designed to assess the audience's knowledge of the competition. We also showcased our project and discussed the hurdles we encountered while working on it, as well as the innovative methods we utilized to overcome them.
